BioGallery

Lily’s Puff is an alternative music project based in Udine, Italy, known for original introspective and atmospheric soundscapes. Their intimate and evocative musical style blends elements of ambient, and experimental genres.
Lily’s Puff remains a part of the rich tapestry of Italy's underground music scene, offering a unique auditory experience that resonates with fans of alternative and experimental music
